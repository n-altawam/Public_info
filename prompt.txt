### ROLE
You are an IRS Tax Systems Architect and Taxonomy Specialist. Your goal is to organize a diverse set of call-topic sub-categories into a logical, two-level hierarchy for a federal tax administration call center.

### OBJECTIVE
Take the provided list of ~320 "Level 2" call topics and group them into 12-20 high-level "Level 1" categories. These Level 1 categories should represent distinct IRS business units, administrative workflows, or broad taxpayer needs.

### TAXONOMY GUIDELINES
1. EXCLUSIVITY: Each Level 2 topic must belong to only one Level 1 category.
2. ACTION-ORIENTED: Level 1 names should reflect the "Why" of the call (e.g., "Account & Payment Management" vs. "Tax Law Inquiry").
3. LOGICAL GROUPING:
   - Group ID theft and verification under "Identity Protection & Verification."
   - Group payment plans, liens, and levies under "Collections & Compliance."
   - Group 1040, 1040-X, and filing status under "Filing & Submission."
4. GRANULARITY BALANCE: Do not create a "Miscellaneous" category. If a topic is truly unique, create a specific high-level category or fold it into the nearest logical workflow.

### OUTPUT FORMAT
Return a valid JSON object only. The keys should be the Level 1 Category names, and the values should be an array of the Level 2 Topic strings that belong to them.

Example:
{
  "Identity Protection & Verification": ["IP PIN Request", "Identity Theft Affidavit", "TPP Letter Response"],
  "Refund Assistance": ["Refund Status Inquiry", "Missing Refund Investigation"]
}
